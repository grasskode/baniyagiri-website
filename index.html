BBaniyagiri

Expense sharing without the spreadsheet.

Track what you spent, who you spent it with, and who owes whom — without drowning in receipts.

Baniyagiri is a mobile app for splitting expenses with friends, family, and groups. Add purchases as they happen, import bank statements when you are catching up, and let the app sort the categories so you can see where the month went.

What it does

  • Split expenses with friends and groups
  • Settle balances with one tap
  • Bulk-import from CSV or PDF bank statements
  • Auto-categorise spending

An Android app. iOS will follow.
